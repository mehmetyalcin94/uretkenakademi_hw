HOW I STARTED

First Kart, Dec 7, 1998

It was fun!

My father was a professional racer. I grew up watching him race and I wanted to do it as well. When I told my father that I want to race, he bought me my first kart. That is how I started.


WHERE I AM

Retirement, 2029

It was time..

After a long career I decided to retire. I had so much fun and I had accomplished more than I have imagined so I have no regrets. I still miss racing but it was right thing to do.
